Yes, there are assessments in the courses offered on our online learning portal. These assessments are designed to evaluate your understanding of the course material and track your progress. Once you have completed an assessment, the instructor will review your submissions and evaluate your performance. The assessment scores will be updated in your "Completed Assessments" tab, allowing you to track your progress and see how you performed in each assessment. The assessments play a crucial role in assessing your comprehension of the course content and provide valuable feedback for your learning journey. They serve as checkpoints to gauge your understanding and ensure that you are grasping the concepts effectively.
